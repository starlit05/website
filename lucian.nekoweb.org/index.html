january 12

fell asleep too early last night. 4pm -> 11pm meant roughly 7 hours of sleep. i ended up staying up until dawn and had to wake my mom up to remind her i had to go to school. it was a tiring day. i was needlessly cruel to my math teacher. i felt very guilty afterwards, but guilt doesn't do anything, at least not directly. i had bad thoughts through the night since i couldn't sleep. i kept thinking about this relationship that i ruined a few months ago. i can't decide if i wish that i could communicate better, so this never happened, or if i wish that i never communicated (including lashing out) at all. for most of my life, the latter has been the winning option, and yet i logically know that thats Not Good For Me.
